🎯 Problem Statement :-
A leading retail company wants to better understand customer shopping behavior to improve sales, customer satisfaction, and long-term loyalty.

The analysis aims to answer:
How can the company leverage consumer data to identify trends, improve engagement, and optimize marketing strategies?

🛠️ Tools & Technologies Used
🐍 Python (Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, Seaborn)
🗄️ SQL (Data extraction & transformation)
📊 Power BI (Dashboard & visualization)
📁 Jupyter Notebook


📂 Dataset Description
Customer_ID – Unique identifier for each customer
Age – Customer age
Gender – Gender of the customer
Category – Product category purchased
Item_Purchased – Specific product purchased
Purchase_Amount – Total purchase value
Location – Customer location
Size – Product size (if applicable)
Color – Product color
Season – Season of purchase (Winter, Summer, etc.)
Review_Rating – Customer rating for the product
Subscription_Status – Whether customer is subscribed
Payment_Method – Mode of payment
Shipping_Type – Type of delivery
Discount_Applied – Indicates if discount was applied
Promo_Code_Used – Whether promo code was used
Previous_Purchases – Number of past purchases
Preferred_Payment_Method – Customer’s preferred payment type
Frequency_of_Purchases – Purchase frequency

🔍 Key Analysis Performed
    Data Cleaning & Preprocessing
    Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
    Customer Segmentation (New, Returning, Loyal)
    Category-wise purchase trends
    Impact of discounts and reviews on sales
    Online vs Offline behavior analysis
    Payment method preferences

📈 Key Insights
    Identified high-value customer segments
    Found strong correlation between discounts and purchase volume
    Seasonal trends significantly impact sales
    Certain payment methods are preferred for repeat purchases
    Online channels show different behavior compared to offline

📊 Power BI Dashboard

The dashboard includes:
   1. Sales trends by category
   2. Customer segmentation analysis
   3. Channel-wise performance
   4. Interactive filters for deep insights

🚀 Business Impact
Helps in targeted marketing strategies
Improves customer engagement
Supports data-driven decision making
Enhances sales and customer retention
